Masonry

Stone

Stonework is a popular type of masonry typically used as decoration similar to things such as siding. Although it can be structural, it is more practical to use something like block or brick then stone because of the variation in each stone. It usually involves either full stone being stacked on top of each other and tied into the wall, or using thin stone and sticking it to a wall.
